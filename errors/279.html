Error 279: Connection Attempt Failed

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

Roblox could not connect you to the game server after multiple attempts. This is one of the most common Roblox errors and is almost always related to your network or Roblox server availability.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Roblox servers are down or overloaded
  • 2 Your firewall is blocking Roblox connection ports
  • 3 Your internet connection is too slow or unstable to reach the server
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Check status.roblox.com first — if servers are down, wait it out
  • 2 Add Roblox to your firewall whitelist and try again
  • 3 Restart your router, wait 60 seconds, and rejoin
Still not fixed? Contact Roblox official support for further assistance
