Error 272: Exploit Program Detected

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

Roblox detected that you attempted to join a game while running an exploit or cheat program. The connection was blocked to protect the game and its players.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 An exploit program is open on your device
  • 2 A legitimate tool is being falsely detected as an exploit
  • 3 Residual files from a previously uninstalled exploit program
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Close all programs except Roblox and your browser, then rejoin
  • 2 Reinstall Roblox and restart your device
  • 3 If you are not using exploits, scan your device for malware and try again
Still not fixed? Contact Roblox official support for further assistance
