Error 918: Xbox Unknown Error

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

This error is exclusive to Xbox and occurs for reasons that are not fully documented. It typically indicates an account-level configuration issue on the Xbox platform.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Unknown Xbox-specific account configuration issue
  • 2 Incompatibility between Xbox account type and Roblox features
  • 3 Rare platform-level error on Xbox
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Restart the Roblox app on Xbox completely
  • 2 Sign out of your Xbox account and sign back in, then relaunch Roblox
  • 3 Contact Roblox Support with your Xbox account details for specific assistance
