Error 0271: Matchmaking Failure (Type 6)

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

Error 271 is triggered when the analytics subsystem encounters an unexpected state. Players may be disconnected or unable to proceed until the issue resolves.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Corrupted local Roblox files
  • 2 VPN or proxy service interfering
  • 3 Roblox CDN experiencing issues
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Update the Roblox client from roblox.com
  • 2 Reinstall Roblox completely
  • 3 Restart Roblox and try again
