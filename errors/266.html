Error 266: Connection Timed Out

Updated 2026
💡 What Is Roblox Error 266?

Roblox Error 266 means your connection to the game server timed out. According to Roblox's official support page, this happens when the client application tries to establish a connection with Roblox servers but times out due to a connectivity issue such as low bandwidth or an unresponsive internet connection. The game stopped responding for too long and was disconnected.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Low bandwidth — your internet speed is too slow to maintain a stable Roblox connection
  • 2 Your connection became unresponsive or idle for an extended period while in-game
  • 3 Network congestion on your router or ISP causing high latency and packet loss
✅ How to Fix Error 266 — Step by Step
  • 1 Check your internet speed at fast.com or speedtest.net. Roblox requires at least 4-8 Mbps for stable gameplay. If your speed is lower, contact your ISP or upgrade your plan.
  • 2 Close all other applications and browser tabs that are using the internet while you play Roblox. Video streaming, downloads, and other games compete for bandwidth.
  • 3 Move closer to your Wi-Fi router or switch to a wired Ethernet connection. Distance from the router significantly reduces connection speed and stability.
  • 4 Restart your router and modem. Unplug them for 30 seconds, then reconnect. This often clears bandwidth throttling and connection timeouts.
  • 5 If the problem happens repeatedly in one specific game, the game itself may have a script that causes disconnections — try a different game to confirm.
