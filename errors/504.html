Error 504: Gateway Timeout

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

A Roblox gateway server timed out while waiting for a response from an upstream server. This can be caused by maintenance, high traffic, or connection problems.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Roblox maintenance or server overload
  • 2 Your internet connection is too slow to reach the server in time
  • 3 Temporary shutdown of a specific Roblox service
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Check status.roblox.com for maintenance announcements
  • 2 Test your internet connection speed — slow connections can trigger this error
  • 3 Wait and try again — gateway timeouts are usually temporary
