Error 106: Cross-Platform Friends Error

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

You are trying to join a friend on a different platform but your account settings do not allow cross-platform play. This is a privacy and account settings issue, not a connection problem.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Cross-platform play is disabled in account settings
  • 2 Friend request not accepted properly across platforms
  • 3 Account age restrictions blocking cross-platform features
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Log in to roblox.com and go to Settings, then Privacy, and enable cross-platform play
  • 2 Ask your friend to accept your friend request on their platform
  • 3 If you are under 13, a parent may need to update privacy settings
Still not fixed? Contact Roblox official support for further assistance
