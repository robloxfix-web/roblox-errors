Error 278: Idle Disconnect — 20 Minutes

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

You were disconnected because you were idle in the game for 20 minutes or more. This is an intentional Roblox feature designed to free up server space from inactive players.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 You left the game running without actively playing
  • 2 You were AFK for more than 20 consecutive minutes
  • 3 This is by design — not a bug or error
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 This is normal behavior — simply rejoin the game when you are ready to play
  • 2 Stay active in the game to prevent being kicked for idling
  • 3 There is no way to disable this feature — it applies to all Roblox games
