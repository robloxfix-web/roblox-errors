Error 0262: Remote Connection Lost

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

Error 262 occurs when the remote connection between your client and the server is lost unexpectedly. You are disconnected from the session.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Internet connection dropped briefly
  • 2 Server crashed or restarted
  • 3 ISP routing issue causing packet loss
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Check your internet and rejoin
  • 2 Restart your router if connectivity issues persist
  • 3 Switch to a wired connection for stability
