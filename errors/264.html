Error 264: Same Account on Multiple Devices

Updated 2026
💡 What Is Roblox Error 264?

Roblox Error 264 occurs when your account is used to launch a game on more than one device at the same time. Roblox does not allow the same account to play simultaneously across different devices. When a second device launches a game, the first device receives Error 264 and is disconnected.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 You opened Roblox on a second device while still playing on another
  • 2 A family member or friend used your account credentials on their device
  • 3 A previous Roblox session on another device did not close properly
✅ How to Fix Error 264 — Step by Step
  • 1 Close Roblox on all other devices. Make sure no other phone, tablet, or computer is running Roblox with your account, then rejoin the game on the device you want to use.
  • 2 Log out of all devices through the Roblox website. Go to roblox.com → Settings → Security → Log out of all other sessions.
  • 3 If you did not log in on another device, change your Roblox password immediately. Someone else may be using your account without your knowledge.
  • 4 Enable two-step verification on your account at roblox.com/my/account for extra security to prevent unauthorized access.
  • 5 After securing your account, wait 2-3 minutes before rejoining any game to ensure all previous sessions have fully closed.
