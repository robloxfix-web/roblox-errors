Error 277: Connection Lost

Updated 2026
💡 What Is This Error?

Your connection to the Roblox game server was lost while you were playing. The game client could not maintain the connection and you were disconnected.

⚠️ Common Causes
  • 1 Unstable or weak Wi-Fi connection
  • 2 Roblox server experienced a temporary crash or restart
  • 3 Firewall or ISP blocking the Roblox connection mid-session
✅ How to Fix It — Step by Step
  • 1 Rejoin the game immediately — this is often a temporary drop
  • 2 Switch from Wi-Fi to a wired Ethernet connection for better stability
  • 3 Check status.roblox.com to see if there is a platform outage
Still not fixed? Contact Roblox official support for further assistance
